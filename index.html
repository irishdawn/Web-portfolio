Growth & Insights

My path of continuous learning and development

AI Academy – Ethics in Artificial Intelligence

I learned the importance of using AI responsibly and ethically. This includes avoiding misinformation such as fake news and deepfakes, and understanding how AI can negatively impact others when misused. I also realized that over-reliance on AI can reduce critical thinking and independent learning, so it is important to use AI as a support tool rather than a replacement for personal understanding.

Understanding Responsible AI Use

I developed awareness on how AI outputs should always be checked for accuracy and reliability. This helped me understand the importance of verifying information before using or sharing it, especially in academic and real-world contexts.

AI Prompting Skills

I learned how to write clear and structured prompts to help AI tools better understand user intent. This improved the accuracy of responses and made AI-assisted tasks more effective and useful in my academic work.

Canva Design with AI Integration

I used ChatGPT and Canva together to improve my design outputs. I combined AI-generated suggestions with my own decisions in layout, color, and structure, which helped me create clearer, more organized, and visually effective presentations while still maintaining my personal creativity.
